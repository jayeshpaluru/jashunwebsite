About Me

Hi there! I'm Jashun Paluru, a driven and accomplished student at Rice University pursuing a Bachelor of Sciences degree in Computer Science and Biochemistry, with a minor in Financial Computation and Modeling. I'm excited to graduate in May 2026 with a diverse skill set that includes Analytical Chemistry, Fundamentals of Cellular and Molecular Neuroscience, Organic Chemistry Lab, Computational Thinking, and Introduction to Real Analysis. During my External Coursework at Purdue University from August 2021 to May 2022, I was thrilled to excel in courses such as Organic Chemistry I & II, Physical Chemistry, and Ordinary Differential Equations, achieving a perfect 4.0 GPA. At William Henry Harrison High School, I was Valedictorian and graduated with a 4.7/4.0 GPA. I even achieved impressive SAT and ACT scores of 1590 and 36, respectively. My high school coursework is relevant to my current studies and includes a variety of AP courses such as Biology, Physics C: Mechanics, Physics C: Electromagnetism, Multivariable Calculus, Linear Algebra, Calculus BC, Statistics, English Literature, English Language, and I even placed third at the Scripps National Spelling Bee. Beyond my academic accomplishments, I'm proud to have gained professional experience as a researcher at Purdue University, where I worked under Dr. Clark Gedney & Dr. Seema Mattoo from November 2019 to May 2022. During this time, I executed 3 research projects in Cellular & Molecular Biology over ~2200 hours, focusing on drug discovery, post-translational modifications, protein interactions, and cellular structure. I'm particularly proud to have co-authored “A Fluorescence Polarization-Based High-Throughput Screen to Identify the First Small-Molecule Modulators of the Human Adenylyltransferase HYPE/FICD” and summarized & presented findings at monthly Undergraduate Research Conferences at Purdue University and weekly Lab Meetings. My work was recognized by Society for In Vitro Biology for Outstanding Work. Outside of academics, I have demonstrated leadership and involvement in various organizations such as North South Foundation, Food Finders Food Bank, Boy Scouts, and National Honor Society. I have a lot of experience when it comes to various skills such as Cell Culture, SDS-PAGE, Western Blotting, Transformation, Protein Purification, Transfection, Fluorescence Assays, PCR, ImageJ, Excel, Python, R, Data Visualization, Presentation & Communication, and I am fluent in Telugu and conversational in Spanish. In my free time, I enjoy camping, cooking, and running. I can't wait to see where my passions and talents will take me in the future!
